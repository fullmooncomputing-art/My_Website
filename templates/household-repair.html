Auto-emergency detection

Three triggers. Instant priority dispatch.

When any of these issues is selected, the platform auto-flags the booking as an emergency — 1.5× surge on labour, priority dispatch, and a same-day guarantee.

🔥 Gas Leak

Regulator / Pipe Leak

Trigger: issue_type == regulator_leak

  • Turn gas cylinder valve OFF immediately
  • Open all windows and doors
  • Do NOT switch any electrical appliance on or off
  • Do NOT light any flame
  • Evacuate if the smell is very strong
⚡ Gas-certified tech dispatched · same-day guarantee · platform agent monitors live
💧 Pipe Burst

Burst Pipe — Water Emergency

Trigger: issue_type == pipe_burst

  • Turn off the main water supply valve immediately
  • Move valuables away from the affected area
  • Do not use electrical switches if water is near wiring
  • Document the burst with a photo for the plumber
  • Open taps to drain remaining pressure
⚡ Priority plumber dispatched · urgency forced · 1.5× surge shown at booking
⚡ Power Out

Complete Flat Power Failure

Trigger: issue_type == no_power_whole_flat

  • Check the main MCB/fuse box — trip may have caused the outage
  • If MCB has tripped, reset once — if it trips again, stop
  • Confirm whether neighbours have power (building fault vs your flat)
  • Keep fridge closed — food stays safe for ~4 hours
  • Electrician will inspect MCB, load distribution & wiring
⚡ Licensed electrician dispatched · 30-min target response (T1/T2)
How we protect you

Safety built into every job

🔐

OTP Verification — Start & End

Every job is bookended by two one-time passwords — one to start work and one to confirm completion. 3 wrong entries → platform agent alerted.

  • Pro enters Start-OTP on arriving at your door
  • Job clock starts; no billable time before OTP
  • Any extra work needs in-app approval from you
  • End-OTP confirms satisfaction; escrow released
📸

Photo Evidence at Every Step

Professionals upload photos at key milestones. All stored immutably against the booking — protects both sides in any dispute.

  • Before-photo documents existing condition
  • Parts purchased: receipt + item photo logged
  • Daily progress photo for multi-day jobs
  • After-photo triggers end-OTP eligibility
🏦

Escrow Payment Protection

Your money is held on the platform and released only when you confirm the job is done. 48-hour dispute window after End-OTP.

  • Deposit (20–30%) collected at booking confirmation
  • Balance held in escrow during the job
  • Auto-release at 48 hrs if no dispute raised
  • 100% refund on cancellation before Start-OTP
