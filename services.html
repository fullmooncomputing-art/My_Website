Every Local Service, Now Online

We take India's street vendors and local trades virtual — and help them sell, reach and grow

From the chaiwala on the corner to the doodhwala at your door, India's local vendors power everyday life — yet most are invisible online. Full Moon Computing gives every vendor a simple micro virtual platform: an online presence, digital payments, and a way for customers to find, order and pay — boosting their trade without changing how they work.

How We Take Your Service Virtual

📱

Your Own Mini Website

A clean, mobile-first page for your stall or shop — your products, photos, timings and location, shareable with a single link.

💸

UPI & QR Payments

Accept digital payments with a QR code and instant receipts — no more "no change" problems or cash-only limits.

💬

Order on WhatsApp

Let customers order, book and reorder over WhatsApp, and broadcast daily offers to your regulars in one tap.

📋

Online Catalog & Prices

Show your products and update daily prices online, so customers always know what's fresh and available.

🔁

Subscriptions & Bookings

Recurring orders (milk, tiffin, boxes) and appointment booking (salon, tailor, repair) handled automatically.

📍

Get Found Locally

Show up on Google Maps and local search with a real brand identity, so new customers discover you every day.
