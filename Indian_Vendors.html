Most vulnerable
Newspaper Hawker and Mochi face structural decline. Digital news and cheap imports are eroding their trade — they need active pivoting strategies.
Highest scaling potential
Mobile Repair and Chaiwala lead scalability. Proven franchise plays like Chai Sutta Bar and iRepair networks are already validating the model.
Most seasonal risk
Patang Wala and Mehndiwali earn up to 90% of annual income in just 2–5 months, leaving them financially exposed the rest of the year.
Biggest digital gap
Kisan Vendor, Feri Wala, and Kabadiwala are almost entirely offline — yet have real use cases for price discovery and route optimization tools.
